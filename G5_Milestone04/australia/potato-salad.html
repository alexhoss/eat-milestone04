Potato Salad

  • Prep Time: 20m
  • Cook Time: 30m
  • Total Time: 50m
  • Serves: 7

Ingredients

  • 6 Large Potatoes, Rinsed in Cool Water
  • 1/2 Cup Fresh Parsley, Chopped
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon Fresh Cilantro, Chopped
  • 1 Teaspoon Deli-style Mustard
  • 1/2 Cup Low-fat Mayonnaise
  • 2 Hard-boiled Eggs, Chopped
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Gently boil potatoes until a skewer will penetrate to the centre.
  2. Turn off heat, pour off water and fill the pot with cold water.
  3. Leave until potatoes are quite cold.
  4. Lift from water and peel off outer skin of potatoes.
  5. Chop potatoes into even sized cubes and place into a bowl with onion, parsley and cilantro.
  6. In small bowl mix together the mustard and mayonnaise.
  7. Add to potatoes with chopped eggs, and desired amount of salt and pepper.
  8. Mix gently to combine.
  9. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
