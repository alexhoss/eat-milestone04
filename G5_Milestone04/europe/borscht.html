Borscht

  • Prep Time: 15m
  • Cook Time: 40m
  • Total Time: 55m
  • Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded beets
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cans beef broth
  • 2 cups chopped cabbage
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice

Directions

  1. In a big pot cook the beets, carrots, and onions in the beef broth for 20 minutes.
  2. Add the cabbage and butter, cook for another 20 minutes.
  3. Stir in lemon juice and season to taste.
  4. Let sit for 1 hour, and enjoy!
