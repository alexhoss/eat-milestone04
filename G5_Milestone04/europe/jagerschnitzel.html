Jagerschnitzel

  • Prep Time: 20m
  • Cook Time: 30m
  • Total Time: 50m
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 cutlets, veal
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 egg, beaten with 1 sp water
  • 1 cup baby bellas mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Cabernet
  • 1/2 cup beef stock
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200F
  2. Pound veal with a mallet to 1/4 inch thickness
  3. Cover the veal in flower, then the egg mixture, lastly the bread crumbs.
  4. Set a pan to medium-hot heat, and add oil to it.
  5. Once oil is simmering, cook the veal on one side until brown, then flip.
  6. Place veal in over to keep warm. Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
  7. Pour out pan drippings, but keep a small layer for cooking the mushrooms.
  8. Cook mushrooms until soft. Remove from heat and add wine.
  9. Deglaze the pan by scraping the bits. Put back on medium heat.
  10. Add 1 Tbsp flour and whisk in. Allow the roux to turn into a bronze colour.
  11. Whisk in cream, stock and turn heat to high. It is important to keep whisking, whisk until desired thickness.
  12. Plate and serve.
