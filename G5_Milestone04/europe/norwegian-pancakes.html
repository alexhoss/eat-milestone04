Norwegian Pancakes

  • Prep Time: 5m
  • Cook Time: 10m
  • Total Time: 15m
  • Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 1 cup flower
  • 1/4 Tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Combine eggs and milk in large bowl
  2. Add flour, sald and sugar
  3. Blend using hand-held mixer or wisk, until smooth.
  4. Heat a skillit over medium-high heat and coat with oil.
  5. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto skillit and tilt the pan in a circular motion to cover pan with batter.
  6. Cook until the top looks dry then flip to the other side for acouple seconds.
  7. Remove from plate and enjoy.
