Perogies

  • Prep Time: 1h30m
  • Cook Time: 30m
  • Total Time: 2h
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients

    Filling:
  • 1 1/2 pounds potatoes
  • 2 Tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/3 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • Pepper to liking
    • Dough:
  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup water

Directions

    Filling:
  1. Place all peeled potatoes in water. Bring to a boil and cook until tender.
  2. While potatoes are cooking, bring a pan to medium-high heat and brown onions and garlic.
  3. Drain and mash potatoes. Add in onions, garlic, cream cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside to cool.
    Dough:
  1. In a large bowl add flower and salt. Make a hole in the flour and add sour cream, egg and water. Mix together with a spoon.
  2. Place dough on a floured surface. Knead the dough until smooth. Set aside and cover dough with towel for 10 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough up into thirds. Roll out the dough until it is 1/8" thick. Cut into 3" circles, keeping access dough for more circles.
  4. Fill each circle with a spoon full of potatoes. Fold the circle into a half circle.Take a fork and press down edges.
  5. Place perogies in boiling water, stir to keep them from sticking to each other. Cook until they rise to the top. Place on plate and enjoy.
