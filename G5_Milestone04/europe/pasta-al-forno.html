Pasta Al Forna

  • Prep Time: 20m
  • Cook Time: 30m
  • Total Time: 50m
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 450g rigatoni pasta
  • 4 Tbsp virgin olive oil
  • 10 large sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 dried chile peppers, crushed
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 jar tomato puree
  • Salt
  • 400g grated mozzarella
  • Pamigiano cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Cook rigatoni in boiling water, while that is happening prepare the sauce.
  3. In a saucepan heat up olive oil and add sun-dried tomates, chile peppers, and garlic. Cook until sauted
  4. Add tomatoe puree and salt. Cook for 10 mins on medium heat.
  5. Drain rigatoni and add to sauce pan. Stir rigatoni and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Empty 1/2 saucepan into a baking dish and add 1/2 mozzarela and 1/2 pamigiano. Add the rest of the pasta and add a layer of remaining cheese.
  7. Bake 20 to 30 minutes, or until golden brown on top.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes.
