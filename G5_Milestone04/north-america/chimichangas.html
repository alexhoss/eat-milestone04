Chimichangas

  • Prep Time: 20m
  • Cook Time: 25m
  • Total Time: 45m
  • Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Salsa
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 0.5 Tsp Crushed Oregano Leaves
  • 1.5 Cups Chopped Chicken
  • 2 Chopped Green onions
  • 6 Flour Tortillas
  • 2 Tbs Margarine
  • 1 Cup Shredded Cheese
  • 0.5 Cup Picante Sauce

Directions

  1. Mix chicken, picante sauce or salsa, cumin, oregano, cheese and onions.
  2. Place about 1/4 cup of the chicken mixture in the center of each tortilla.
  3. Fold opposite sides over filling.
  4. Roll up from bottom and place seam-side down on a baking sheet..
  5. Brush with margarine and bake at 400F for 25 minutes
  6. Garnish with cheese, onions, and salsa
